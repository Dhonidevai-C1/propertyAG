## 🗣️ The Pitch Script (Offline Meeting)

Use this flow when sitting with a dealer:

---

### Step 1 — The Hook (30 seconds)
> *"Tell me honestly — when a client calls you and says 'I want a 3BHK under 60 lakhs in Vaishali Nagar', how long does it take you to figure out which properties you have that match?"*

**Let them answer.** They'll say: *"2-3 minutes"*, *"I have to check Excel"*, *"I message my team"*, etc.

> *"What if I told you I can make that happen in 2 seconds — automatically?"*

---

### Step 2 — The Demo (5 minutes)
Show them these 3 things **in this exact order** — they are the most impressive:

1. **The Smart Match** — Add a fake client, hit "Run Match" → show them properties scored 85/100, 72/100, 60/100. This is your WOW moment.
2. **The Property Gallery** — Show a property with photos, price, all details on one screen.
3. **The Team Dashboard** — Show the activity feed "Rahul added Modern Villa at 75L" — show that an owner can see what all agents are doing.

---

### Step 3 — Paint the Picture
> *"Right now, you're probably juggling between WhatsApp groups, Excel, and your head. When you have 5 agents, that becomes chaos. Client says they were contacted twice for the same property. Properties get missed. Deals get lost.*
>
> *PropDesk gives your entire agency one screen. Every property. Every client. Every match. Live."*

---

### Step 4 — Handle Objections

**"It's too expensive for me."**
> *"How much does one missed deal cost you? If I save you even one deal a year because your agent remembered to follow up on time — PropDesk has paid for itself 10 times over."*

**"I'm not tech-savvy."**
> *"Your agents use WhatsApp. They can use this. It works on phone and computer. I'll set it up for you and train your team in 30 minutes."*

**"I'll think about it."**
> *"Absolutely. But let me leave you with this — your competitors are already using systems like this. The ones who're not, are the ones losing clients to dealers who can respond faster."*

**"I can manage with Excel."**
> *"Excel can't call you when a new property matches a client's budget. Excel can't tell your whole team at the same time. Excel can't send your client a professional-looking property sheet. PropDesk does all three, automatically."*

---

### Step 5 — The Close
> *"I'm offering you a 30-day free trial. No credit card. If you don't feel it's saving you time in one month, you stop. But if it does — and I guarantee it will — it's [₹X] per month. Less than one client visit."*
